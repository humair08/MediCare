A Legacy of Healing & Excellence

MediCare Hospital was founded in 1999 with a singular vision: to provide every patient — regardless of background — with the highest quality medical care available anywhere in the world.

From a 50-bed facility to a comprehensive 500-bed hospital complex, we've grown by staying true to our founding values of compassion, innovation, and clinical excellence.

🏆
JCI Accreditation

Internationally recognised for meeting the highest global standards of patient care and safety.

🔬

Quality management systems that ensure consistent, reliable and measurable patient outcomes.

🌍
WHO Recognised Centre of Excellence

One of only 12 hospitals globally recognised for our infectious disease research programme.

Medical Departments

Eight world-class departments equipped with the most advanced medical technology and led by internationally trained specialists.

❤️

Cardiology Department

Our cardiology department is at the forefront of heart disease prevention, diagnosis and treatment. Featuring 3 state-of-the-art cardiac catheterisation labs, 2 EP labs, and a dedicated cardiac ICU with 20 beds. We offer full services from stress testing to complex interventional procedures.

42 Cardiologists 8,200+ Patients / Year 95.2% Success Rate Angioplasty • Bypass • Valve Repair
🧠

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Advanced neurological care covering the full spectrum from epilepsy and migraines to complex brain tumors and spinal cord injuries. Equipped with 3T MRI, intraoperative MRI, and advanced neurophysiology labs for precise diagnosis.

28 Neurologists 5,400+ Patients / Year 97.1% Success Rate
🦴

Orthopedics & Sports Medicine

Comprehensive musculoskeletal care from total joint replacements to minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures. Our sports medicine program serves professional athletes and recreational enthusiasts alike, with cutting-edge regenerative therapies.

35 Orthopedic Surgeons 9,100+ Patients / Year 98.4% Success Rate
👶

Pediatrics & Child Health

A dedicated child-friendly environment providing comprehensive healthcare from newborns to adolescents. Our neonatal ICU is a level 3 centre, supporting the most complex premature births with a team of 8 neonatologists available 24/7.

31 Pediatricians 12,000+ Patients / Year 99.1% Satisfaction
